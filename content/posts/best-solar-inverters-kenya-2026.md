## üí° Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Can a 3kW Inverter run a Microwave?
Technically yes, but we don't recommend it for long. A microwave draws about 1200W-1500W. On a 3kW system, this is 50% of your capacity. If your fridge kicks in at the same time, the inverter might trip.

### Which battery is best for these inverters?
In 2026, **LiFePO4 (Lithium)** is the way to go. While Tubular batteries are cheaper upfront, Lithium lasts 10 years, whereas Tubular often fails after 2 years in Kenyan heat.
