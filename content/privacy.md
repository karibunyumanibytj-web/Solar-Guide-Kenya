At **Solar Guide Kenya**, we believe in being 100% honest with our readers. This page explains how we make money and how we protect your information.

### 1. Affiliate Disclosure
Solar Guide Kenya is a participant in several affiliate programs (including Jumia and Amazon). This means that **at no additional cost to you**, we may earn a small commission if you click on a link and make a purchase.

**Why do we do this?**
These commissions help pay for our website hosting and the time we spend researching solar equipment. We only recommend products (like Must, Growatt, and Deye) that we believe offer real value to Kenyan homeowners.

### 2. Privacy Policy
We value your privacy. In accordance with the **Kenya Data Protection Act**:
- **Personal Information:** If you contact us via WhatsApp or Email, we only use your details to answer your questions. We never sell your data.
- **Cookies:** Our website may use cookies to understand how visitors use the site so we can make it better.

### 3. Professional Advice
While we are solar enthusiasts and experts, the information on this site is for educational purposes. Always consult a certified solar technician before performing any electrical installations in your home.
